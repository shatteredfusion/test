FAQs

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does a Rillet implementation take with EchoPark?

Most of our clients go live in 4–8 weeks. The exact timeline depends on complexity—number of entities, integrations required, volume of historical data to migrate, and how quickly your team can participate in UAT sessions. We'll give you a specific timeline estimate in your discovery call.

Do we need to already have Rillet to work with EchoPark?

No. We work with companies at any stage—from early evaluation through to post-launch optimization. If you're still deciding whether Rillet is right for you, we're happy to help you evaluate it. We only recommend implementations that we genuinely believe will serve you well.

What does EchoPark charge for an implementation?

Our pricing is transparent and project-based—you'll know exactly what you're paying and what you're getting before we start. Cost varies based on complexity: number of legal entities, integration requirements, data migration complexity, and post-launch support needs. Book a discovery call and we'll give you a ballpark in our first conversation.

We're on NetSuite. Is switching to Rillet the right move?

It depends on your situation. Rillet is particularly well-suited for SaaS and subscription businesses that need strong revenue recognition, real-time reporting, and AI-powered automation. Many of our clients have migrated from NetSuite and seen significant improvements in close time, reporting speed, and team satisfaction. We'll give you an honest assessment on your discovery call.

What happens after go-live? Do you disappear?

Absolutely not. Post-launch support is a core part of what we offer. We stay available for questions, help your team during the first few close cycles, support new employee onboarding, and assist with rolling out new Rillet features as they're released. We never just hand you the keys and walk away.

Are you able to work with companies outside the US?

Yes. While our team is based in Los Angeles, we work with clients across North America and can accommodate international implementations depending on scope. Reach out and let's discuss your specific situation.
