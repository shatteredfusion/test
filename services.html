Our Services

Everything You Need
to Succeed with Rillet

From first conversation to long-term partnership—EchoPark provides the expertise, structure, and commitment you need at every stage of your Rillet journey.

Service 01

Process Mapping & Discovery

Before we write a single configuration, we invest time learning your business—how you recognize revenue, how your team closes the books, where the bottlenecks live, and what your people actually need from an ERP.

The result is a comprehensive future-state process design that eliminates inefficiencies and is purpose-built for Rillet's capabilities—not a lift-and-shift of your current mess into new software.

  • Current state process documentation
  • Gap analysis and recommendation report
  • Future state workflow design
  • Detailed project plan with milestones
  • Stakeholder alignment workshops
Service 02

System Configuration & Build

This is where Rillet comes to life for your business. Our consultants configure every module to your exact specifications—chart of accounts, revenue recognition policies, AP workflows, approval chains, and integrations with your CRM, billing platform, and banking connections.

No generic templates. Every configuration decision is intentional, documented, and reviewed with your team before build starts.

  • Chart of accounts setup and mapping
  • Revenue recognition rule configuration
  • Approval workflow automation
  • Third-party integration setup (Stripe, Salesforce, and more)
  • Custom reporting and dashboard configuration
  • Multi-entity and consolidation setup
Service 03

Data Migration

Moving your financial history into Rillet is one of the most technically demanding parts of any ERP implementation—and one of the most important to get right. A single migration error can haunt your audit trail for years.

We extract data from legacy systems, transform it to match Rillet's data model, run full reconciliation, and only load to production once every balance ties out to the penny.

  • Data extraction from legacy ERP systems
  • Data cleansing and transformation
  • Historical transaction migration
  • Opening balance verification and reconciliation
  • Vendor, customer, and item master migration
  • Audit-ready migration documentation
Service 04

Testing & Validation

We run comprehensive testing before any user touches production—unit testing of individual configurations, integration testing across connected systems, and parallel run periods where your team processes real transactions in both environments to validate results.

Nothing goes live until every test passes and every stakeholder signs off. That's our standard, not the exception.

  • End-to-end test script development
  • User acceptance testing (UAT) facilitation
  • Integration testing with third-party systems
  • Parallel run coordination and reconciliation
  • Issue tracking and resolution management
  • Go/no-go decision framework
Service 05

Training & Change Management

Software only transforms businesses when people actually use it effectively. That's why we invest heavily in training—not generic click-through demos, but role-specific sessions tailored to how each person on your team will actually use Rillet day to day.

We also manage the change: communication plans, manager enablement, and the organizational support that helps teams embrace new ways of working.

  • Role-specific training curriculum
  • Live sessions (virtual or on-site)
  • Recorded walkthroughs for future hires
  • Quick-reference guides and SOPs
  • Change management communication planning
  • Post-launch helpdesk coverage

Our clients don't lose access to us after implementation. They gain a long-term partner who knows their system inside out.
