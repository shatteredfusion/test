Our Services

Everything You Need to
Succeed with Rillet

From first conversation to post-launch optimization, EchoPark provides the expertise, structure, and partnership you need at every stage of your Rillet journey.

Service 01

Process Mapping & Discovery

Before we write a single configuration, we invest time understanding your business—how you recognize revenue, how your team closes the books, where the bottlenecks live, and what your team actually needs from an ERP.

The result is a comprehensive future-state process design that eliminates inefficiencies and is purpose-built for Rillet's capabilities—not just a lift-and-shift of your current mess into new software.

  • Current state process documentation
  • Gap analysis and recommendation report
  • Future state workflow design
  • Detailed project plan with milestones
  • Stakeholder alignment workshops
Service 02

System Configuration & Build

This is where Rillet comes to life for your business. Our consultants configure every module to your exact specifications—from your chart of accounts and revenue recognition policies to AP workflows, approval chains, and integrations with your CRM, billing platform, and banking connections.

We don't use generic templates. Every configuration decision is intentional, documented, and reviewed with your team before build.

  • Chart of accounts setup and mapping
  • Revenue recognition rule configuration
  • Approval workflow automation
  • Third-party integration setup (Stripe, Salesforce, etc.)
  • Custom reporting and dashboard configuration
  • Multi-entity and consolidation setup
Service 03

Data Migration

Moving your financial history into Rillet is one of the most technically demanding parts of any ERP implementation—and one of the most important to get right. A single data migration error can haunt your audit trail for years.

We extract your historical data from legacy systems, transform it to match Rillet's data model, run full reconciliation, and only load it to production once every balance ties out to the penny.

  • Data extraction from legacy ERP systems
  • Data cleansing and transformation
  • Historical transaction migration
  • Opening balance verification and reconciliation
  • Vendor, customer, and item master migration
  • Audit-ready migration documentation
Service 04

Testing & Validation

We run comprehensive testing before any user touches production. This includes unit testing of individual configurations, integration testing across connected systems, and parallel run periods where your team processes transactions in both the old and new systems to validate results.

Nothing goes live until every test passes and every stakeholder signs off. That's our standard, not the exception.

  • End-to-end test script development
  • User acceptance testing (UAT) facilitation
  • Integration testing with third-party systems
  • Parallel run coordination and reconciliation
  • Issue tracking and resolution management
  • Go/no-go decision framework
Service 05

Training & Change Management

Software only transforms businesses when people actually use it effectively. That's why we invest heavily in training—not generic click-through demos, but role-specific sessions tailored to how each person on your team will actually use Rillet day to day.

We also manage the change: communication plans, manager enablement, and the organizational support structures that help teams embrace new ways of working.

  • Role-specific training curriculum development
  • Live training sessions (virtual or on-site)
  • Recorded video walkthroughs for future hires
  • Quick-reference guides and SOPs
  • Change management communication planning
  • Post-launch helpdesk coverage
Service 06

Ongoing Support & Optimization

Go-live is the beginning of your Rillet journey, not the end of ours. EchoPark remains your dedicated partner long after launch—available for questions, system enhancements, new feature rollouts, new employee onboarding, and continuous optimization as your business grows and changes.

Our clients don't lose access to us after implementation. They gain a long-term partner who knows their system inside out.

  • Dedicated support channel with guaranteed response times
  • Monthly system health reviews
  • New feature adoption and rollout support
  • Additional module implementation
  • New team member training
  • Annual process optimization reviews
