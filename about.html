Our Story

We Believe Transformation
Starts with People

EchoPark was founded on a simple premise: ERP implementations fail when consultants treat software as the solution and people as the variable. We do it the other way around.

Our Mission

Helping Finance Teams Thrive, Not Just Survive

We started EchoPark after watching too many ERP projects go sideways—not because of technology failures, but because consultants showed up with a pre-built playbook and ignored the humans who had to live in the system every day.

When Rillet launched as the first truly AI-native ERP, we recognized it as the platform that finally matched our philosophy: built by accountants, for accountants, with AI woven in from day one rather than bolted on as an afterthought.

So we built the consulting practice that Rillet deserved—one that combines deep technical implementation expertise with genuine commitment to your team's long-term success.

What We Stand For

Our Values

These aren't values we put on a wall—they're commitments that show up in how we work with every client, every day.

01

People Before Software

Technology is only as valuable as the people using it. Every decision we make—from configuration choices to training design—is filtered through one question: will this make your team more effective?

02
